Frequently Asked Questions

What is Count My Words?

Count My Words is a free, online word count tool that helps writers, students, and professionals analyze text efficiently. It offers word counts, character counts, sentence counts, paragraph counts, reading levels, reading times, speaking times, and URL fetching—perfect for improving writing, optimizing SEO, and preparing speeches. It’s fast, intuitive, and completely free to use on any device.

Is Count My Words really free to use?

Yes, Count My Words is entirely free with no hidden costs or subscriptions. You can access all features—like real-time text analysis, URL fetching, and dark mode—without paying a dime. We’re committed to providing a valuable, accessible tool for everyone, ensuring no barriers to improving your writing.

How accurate are the reading level and time estimates?

Our reading level is calculated using a simplified Flesch-Kincaid formula, based on sentence length and word complexity, targeting 7th-8th grade readability for blogs. Reading time assumes 200 words per minute, and speaking time uses 130 words per minute—standard benchmarks. These estimates are highly accurate for general purposes, but individual reading or speaking speeds may vary slightly.

Does Count My Words store my text or personal information?

No, we don’t store your text input. Count My Words processes your data in real-time for analysis and doesn’t save or share it. We only collect non-personal usage analytics (e.g., browser type) to improve our site. Review our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.

Can I use Count My Words on my mobile phone?

Absolutely! Count My Words is fully mobile-friendly, with a responsive design that works on phones and tablets. It features a hamburger menu for navigation, large touch targets, and dark/light mode options. Use it anywhere, anytime, for free, with no app download needed.
