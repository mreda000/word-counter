How to Use Count My Words

Unlock the power of Count My Words, our free online word count tool designed to enhance your writing process. Whether you’re a student crafting essays, a blogger optimizing SEO, or a professional timing speeches, this guide will walk you through using our tool to analyze text effectively and improve your content.

Step 1: Access the Tool

Visit our homepage at Count My Words. You’ll see a dark-mode, mobile-friendly interface with a text area, URL input, and buttons—perfect for quick use on any device. Our sleek design ensures a seamless experience, even on the go.

Step 2: Input Your Text

Type or paste your text into the “Type or paste your text here…” textarea. As you type, Count My Words instantly displays real-time stats: word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, reading level, reading time, and speaking time. Use this to refine sentence length, boost readability, or hit word goals for SEO or assignments.

Step 3: Fetch Text from URLs

Need to analyze a webpage? Enter the URL in the “Enter URL here…” field and click “Fetch & Analyze.” Our tool retrieves the text, providing the same detailed analysis—ideal for bloggers checking competitor content or students reviewing articles. It’s fast, free, and accurate for any online text.

Step 4: Explore Advanced Features

Take advantage of our advanced features: check reading level to ensure accessibility (target 7th-8th grade for blogs), use speaking time (130 words/minute) for presentations, or copy results with “Copy Results.” Clear your input with “Clear” to start fresh. Toggle dark mode for comfort with the “Toggle Dark Mode” button—perfect for long sessions.

Count My Words saves time, improves writing efficiency, and enhances SEO. Visit our blog for tips, or contact us at countmywords.com@gmail.com for support. Start analyzing today and elevate your writing game!
