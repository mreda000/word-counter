About Us

Welcome to Count My Words, your go-to destination for a powerful, free word count tool and text analysis. We’re passionate about helping writers, students, bloggers, and professionals optimize their content with accurate, user-friendly tools. Whether you’re crafting an essay, polishing a blog post, or timing a speech, our platform makes it easy to analyze words, characters, reading levels, and more—right at your fingertips.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower everyone to improve their writing and communication skills through innovative, accessible tools. At Count My Words, we believe that clear, concise content can transform ideas into impact. By offering features like URL text fetching, reading time estimates, and speaking time calculations, we aim to boost productivity and creativity, all while keeping it completely free and intuitive. We’re here to support your writing journey, one word at a time.

Why Choose Us?

  • Seamless, intuitive design for an effortless user experience.
  • Advanced text analysis, including word counts, reading levels, and speech timing.
  • Free access with no hidden fees—perfect for students, writers, and pros alike.
  • Mobile-friendly and dark mode-ready for convenience anytime, anywhere.

Our Story

Count My Words started as a passion project for a group of writers frustrated by clunky word-counting tools. We launched in 2025, driven by the idea that everyone deserves a simple way to analyze and refine their text. Today, we’re growing, adding features like URL fetching and blog tips, all while staying true to our roots—helping you write better, faster, and smarter. Check out our blog for more insights!
