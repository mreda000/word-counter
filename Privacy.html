Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2025

Welcome to Count My Words! Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our free word count tool and visit our website. We’re committed to transparency and ensuring your data stays secure, so you can focus on improving your writing without worry.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data to enhance your experience with our word count tool:

  • Non-personal information, such as browser type, operating system, and website usage analytics, to improve site performance.
  • Personal information (e.g., email addresses) only if you voluntarily submit it through contact forms, email subscriptions, or feedback—rest assured, we never sell or share this data.

2. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to make your browsing smoother and more personalized. These cookies help with analytics, ad targeting (via third-party services like Google AdSense), and remembering your preferences. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but note this may limit some features. We value your choice and transparency in how we use this data.

3. Third-Party Advertisements

We partner with third-party advertising services, including Google AdSense, to display ads on our site. These services may use cookies and similar technologies (like the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to other websites. Google, as a third-party vendor, collects data to provide personalized ads, but you can opt out by visiting the Google Ad Settings page. We strive to balance ad relevance with your privacy, ensuring no sensitive data is shared beyond what’s necessary.

4. Data Security

We take robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure storage practices. However, no online platform is immune to all risks, so we encourage you to be cautious when sharing personal information online. At Count My Words, we never store your text input—only process it in real-time for analysis—ensuring your privacy remains intact.

5. Links to External Sites

Our website may include links to third-party sites, like social media or ad platforms. We’re not responsible for their privacy policies or practices. We recommend reviewing those policies before interacting with external links to protect your data.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here with the new effective date, so check back periodically. Your continued use of Count My Words after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data you’ve shared with us. You can also opt out of non-essential cookies or ad tracking. Contact us at countmywords.com@gmail.com to exercise these rights or ask questions. We’re here to ensure your privacy is respected every step of the way.
